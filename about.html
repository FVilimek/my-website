About - Frank

Frank is a Master's student majoring in Chemical Engineering at the University of California, Davis. He also obtained his B.S. in Chemical Engineering at the University of California, Davis. His senior year capstone project, focusing on designing a small-scale, modular ammonia production plant to be used in the US corn belt, awarded him and his team the Rich Anderson award by the UC Davis Department of Chemical Engineering for having the best senior design project.

Frank loves all things STEM, and outside of Chemical Engineering he has a particular interest in learning about astronomy/astrophysics, anthropology, paleontology, computer science/engineering, and applying mathematics to produce computer simulations of physical systems. Though he has no real training nor has he taken any classes in these subjects, Frank frequently watches educational videos and reads online articles about important topics within them.

In his free time, Frank loves playing video games, and some of his all time favorite video games include Monster Hunter: World, Monster Hunter Wilds, Fallout 3, Fallout: New Vegas, Red Dead Redemption, Red Dead Redemption 2, Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice, Kerbal Space Program, Grand Theft Auto V, and Minecraft, among others.
